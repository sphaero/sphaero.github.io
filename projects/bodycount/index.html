Respons(e)ible Project

How do we relate to the virtual world? In what way can we identify ourselves with and feel responsible for our digital selves? What happens when we cannot control our digital selves? What has been taken away from you? The Resons(e)ible Project is a theatrical research to responsibility in the virtual world. A cooperation of the HKU and z25.org Foundation. We created a theatrical experience in which we copied the audience to the virtual world by using 3d visualization and scanning techniques.

The project was performed at “Soiree des Ateliers” in Huis aan de Werf, Utrecht on 14, 15 & 16 february 2008 and in Frascati, Amsterdam on 11,16,17,18 September 2010.
