Cosmo V Experience

Cosmo V is a friend of mine with quite a succesful release album of ‘bedroompop’ songs. Her music is inspired by artists like Christa PÃ¤ffgen (Nico), Charles Bukowski, Daul Kim and Jim Morisson. For her perfomances we created an experimental immersive environment using curved projections. Artists from z25 experimented with visuals using the songs from Cosmo V.

The visuals were controlled just like a musical instrument and by doing so became a part of the band. Realtime applications like this use a lot of techniques which are used for video games. This is why we chose to put the Blender Game Engine to the test.

Performances were played at:

  • Muziekgebouw, Utrecht
  • DBase, Utrecht
  • Overtoom 301, Amsterdam
  • Tivoli, Utrecht
  • Muziekgebouw aan ‘t Ij/Bimhuis, Amsterdam
