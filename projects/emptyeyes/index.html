Empty Eyes

These sculptures Empty Eyes are designed by Jan Samsom. It was an art assignment for the roundabout near ‘Clinge’ Bussum, The Netherlands. Because of the complexity of the objects a conventional maquette wasn’t suitable. With Jan I created a virtual maquette of the surrounding and made virtual photo’s of it.

At the request of the jury of this project we’ve made pictures of how the objects would look in the environment when finished.
